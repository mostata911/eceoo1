History of operating systems




  • In 1956 the first operating system was established and in the sixties the ibm company was the first company to manufacture computers and began to distribute their operating systems

  • In the fifties, the first operating systems were developed when computers were able to run only one program at a time. After that, more and more programs called libraries were included that were assembled to be an operating system

  • In the late sixties, the first version of the unix operating system was developed and written in the C language and available for free during its early years

  • Many modern operating systems and all different versions of Linux are either dependent on or dependent on the unix operating system
